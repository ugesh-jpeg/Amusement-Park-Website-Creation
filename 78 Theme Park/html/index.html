FAQs

1. Travel

How can I get to 78's Theme Park?

There are various modes of transport to 78's theme park. You can travel by bus, take a car or ride the train.

Buses that head towards 78's Theme Park: 26 , 65 , 188 , 742 .

Train station: Themepark Station NE32.

What are the parking charges at 78 Theme Park?

Parking Rates $35 per car or motorcycle and $40 per oversized vehicle for the entirety of the day.

2. Tour

What time does the theme park open?

78 Theme Park opens on the Weekdays from 8am to 11pm and the Weekends 9am to 12am.

Will the ThemePark be open during wet weather?

Yes the themepark will still be open. However, due to the safety of our guests, some rides will be unavailable.

Have any further questions?
